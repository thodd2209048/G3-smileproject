ORAL-B IO: THE NEXT GENERATION OF OSCILLATING - ROTATING TOOTHBRUSHES

Oral-B iO combines the oscillation-rotating of the iconic round brush head with micro vibrations to deliver a sensational clean that delights patients

Overview

Oscillation rotation and microvibrations for a sensational clean that patients can’t resist.

In over 60 years of continuous innovation to help the world take better care of teeth and gums, we have learned a simple but vital lesson: the more appealing and enjoyable the toothbrush, the more people will use it and the better their results will be.

Made in Germany and designed with dental professionals

Oral-B iO™ is an electric toothbrush range that has been reimagined and redesigned from the inside out. Oral-B iO™ is revolutionary dental technology, a result of insights collected from over six years of product research and development, with input from more than 6000 consumers, and evaluated over more than 43,000 clinical trial study days.

Concentrated energy.

Created from the need to deliver a more effective and enjoyable brushing experience, the iO Linear Magnetic Drive is the heart of the brush. Producing controlled, harmonious oscillation-rotations with gentle micro-vibrations, the frictionless linear magnetic drive

System efficiently concentrates energy at the bristle tips, where it matters most, allowing the brush head to glide tooth by tooth for a smooth, quiet and sensational clean that delivers sensational clean that your patients would love.

Clinically Proven technology with a wealth of scientific evidence

At the heart of every dental professional's practice is the need to make evidence-based recommendations to patients. Since Oral-B developed its first oscillating rotating electric toothbrush in 1991, over 180 studies on its efficacy, gentleness and compliance features have been conducted and many have been published in peer-reviewed dental journals

Redesigned brush heads for every patient need

Oral-B iO™ is compatible with 4 replacement brush heads. The breakthrough Ultimate Clean brush head has been designed with twisted filaments and a high-density tuft-in-tuft configuration, to ensure a deep clean that reaches every contour of the teeth for outstanding whole-mouth plaque removal.

The Gentle Care brush head contains almost 4000 of our finest filaments- the highest filament count and the smallest filament diameter in the Oral-B electric toothbrush range- for a gentle and complete clean your patients will love.
