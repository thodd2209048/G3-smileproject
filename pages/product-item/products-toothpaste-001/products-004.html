The right choice of dentifrice plays a critical role

The mechanical technology of electric toothbrushes with round brush-heads delivers a superior clean and compliance from your patients (vs. ordinary manual toothbrushes). However, patients brush only twice a day, and the left behind plaque keeps growing during the day.

This is where the right choice of dentifrice plays a critical role in protecting patients against a broad range of conditions. and it is clinically proven that a well formulated stabilised Stannous Fluoride toothpaste provides incremental benefits for your patients' plaque control.

Stannous Fluoride in toothpastes - How it works

The benefits of Stannous Fluoride in toothpastes have been well documented in scientific literature. The bacterial plaque reductions observed with the use of Stannous-based Fluoride products are due to the action of Stannous. Stannous is recognized to reduce the stickiness or adhesiveness of dental plaque and also inhibits plaque regrowth in hard-to-read areas. The action of Stannous against plaque lasts for 12 hours.

Stannous Fluoride actions on plaque regrowth

By reducing the ability of bacterial plaque to adhere and to cohere to each other, the thickness and stickiness of dental biofilm is reduced.

Numerous studies using digital plaque imaging analysis have demonstrated this reduction in plaque coverage with use of stabilized Stannous Fluoride toothpaste formulations relative to controls.

Stannous Fluoride offers greater protection than other fluorides against plaque

There is a misconception that ‘all fluorides are the same.’ This is simply not true. Stannous Fluoride stand apart, offering benefits not found with sodium fluoride or sodium monofluorophosphate.

Whilst all three compounds provide anti-caries benefits, Stannous Fluoride, when effectively stabilised, has demonstrated broader and significantly greater protection than other fluorides against plaque, gum problems, halitosis and erosive toothwear.
