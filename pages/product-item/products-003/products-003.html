Overview

No two patients are the same

And each has their own unique clinical needs, motivations and preferences.That’s why dental professionals are in a unique position to help patients personalize their brushing experience to better meet their specific oral health needs, improve compliance with at home care guidance.

Establish habits of changing brush head regularly

To help your patients establish the right habits as of day 1, an interactive brush head replacement reminder is integrated into the Oral-B app. Simply sync your iO with the Oral-B app to track your brush head usage, and the Interactive Display lets you know when it's time for a new brush head.

Selecting oral hygiene aids that meet patients specific clinical or motivational needs

Patients' oral hygiene regimens can be individualized by selecting oral hygiene aids that meet their specific clinical or motivational needs and ensure consistently effective plaque control.

That’s why Oral-B innovates continually with new brush heads so that each of your patients can choose their preferred brushing experience,without compromising on efficacy.
